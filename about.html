About Me

Hey, I’m Timilehin, a 23-year-old from Nigeria who’s having a lot of fun building for the web. I’ve been on this journey for just about six months, diving into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring ideas to life. Whether it’s a landing page, a portfolio, or a mini game, I’m always aiming to create smooth, responsive, and visually clean experiences that work well across devices. I am someone who is always willing to learn new skills and I got curious about web development, gave it a shot, and got hooked. There’s just something satisfying about writing code that turns into a real, usable website. Every project teaches me something new ,and I’m always down to learn more. I’m not a pro yet infact far from it, but I’m consistent, curious, and genuinely passionate about growing. I’ve even started thinking about diving into backend development, just to expand what I can build. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me on a football pitch , either scoring a goal or setting someone else up for one. And when I’m not doing that, I’m gaming, hanging out, or just unwinding. It’s all about learning, having fun, and enjoying the process.
